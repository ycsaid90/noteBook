# English Notes

## Vocabulary

- meantime: in the meantime, meanwhile (mientras tanto)
- beforehand: in advance (de antemano)
- wonder: to want to know something (preguntarse)
- as well as: in addition to (así como)
- choppy: rough, turbulent (agitado) / ex: It is little choppy.
- flawed: having imperfections, not perfect (defectuoso)
- scold: to reprimand or criticize someone (regañar)
